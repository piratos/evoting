About Us

Need help with voting ? You've come to the right place . E_voting is a website dedicated to helping voting act .

It has resources for voters, including voter guides, registration information, voting accessibility, and information for overseas voters; You can register by mail to vote using the National Mail Voter Registration Form.

E-voting is also charged with creating voting system guidelines including several ressources for voters ;here we will help you :

  • Register to Vote : learn how and where to register; and find information on voter eligibility.
  • Find the registration Deadlines
  • Learn about Elections and Voting
  • know "Where Do You Vote"?
    Find your polling location and hours on your governorate election website...

  • Learn more about the candidates :You may want to prepare for Election Day by finding out who's running for office before you go to the voting booth ; here you'll find the needed information

  • Be informed about the election events , where and when you get to participate in the voting process .

Note : e-voting provides web-surfers with a Virtual Public Forum offering a public platform where visitors can view and provide comments on the voting process . If you have any questions about using the Virtual Public Forum, please Contact Us.
